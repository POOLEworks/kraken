Browser Compatability

The web should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their device or browser.

Kraken works in all major browsers, including IE 6. However, it's optimized for modern browsers, and IE 9+.

What does that mean?

Older browsers and less capable devices can still access content, but it might look a bit weird.

For example, because IE 8 and lower do not recognize media queries, they see the single-column small-screen layout instead of the fluid grid.

CSS3 properties like border radius, psuedo-selectors, and drop-shadows on things like buttons and forms aren't recognized by IE 6-8 and some mobile browsers like Opera Mini. Forms and buttons still work in those browsers—they just look a bit simpler.
